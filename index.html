About

Katia Bachko is a writer and editor in New York City. Her work has appeared in The New Yorker, The New York Times, Outside magazine and the Columbia Journalism Review.

She is a graduate of the Columbia Journalism School and New York University. She works at The New Yorker and tweets at @katiabachko.
